Web Design

Any and every website has the potential to be impactful. The secret is the technique in the design. I pride myself on taking special time to ensure that every aspect of a website means something and is not just there to "fill space". A wesbite should make a user experience something, I would like to be a part of creating that experience for you.

Web Development

I am well versed in: HTML5, CSS3, JS, Flask (Python), SQL, SCSS, and Bootstrap. When developing a website, I enjoy utilizing Python's Flask framework most. At heart, I am a programmer; Python's Flask allows me to incorporate front-end as well as back-end development into one project. Meaning, while creating the markup for the website, any related databases, forms, queries, and data extraction your website may need is suddenly possible.

Programming

In the genesis of my degree at Georgia State University, I became well-versed in Java. My exeprience with Java then evolved into Python where I felt like "home" with regard to programming. I began with Python 2, and although subtle changes, pursued the transition into Python 3.0. Feel free to browse a few of my projects in the portfolio section.
